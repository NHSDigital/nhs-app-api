**Welcome to the NHS App**  
  
If you have full access to the NHS App, you can do things like:  
  
- order repeat prescriptions  
- manage appointments  
- view your GP health record  
- get your NHS COVID Pass for travel abroad
  
If you're unable to access these features, you may need to prove who you are. [Read more about how to prove who you are](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/getting-started-with-the-nhs-app/proving-who-you-are-to-get-full-access/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).  

**Get your NHS COVID Pass**

You can view and share your NHS COVID Pass for event trials in England and travel abroad. [Read more about your NHS COVID Pass](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/health-records-in-the-nhs-app/about-the-nhs-covid-pass-service/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).

**GP health record**  
  
If you have access to your GP health record, you should be able to see your summary record. This includes information about your allergies and medicines. To see more information, such as test results, contact your GP surgery to request access to your detailed coded record. [Read more about GP health records](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/health-records-in-the-nhs-app/gp-health-record/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).  
  
**Prescriptions**  
  
View and order your repeat prescriptions. Depending on your GP surgery, you may be able to choose a pharmacy for your prescriptions to be sent to. [Read more about prescriptions](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/prescriptions-in-the-nhs-app/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).  
  
**Appointments**  
  
Book and manage appointments at your GP surgery. Some surgeries have made online appointment booking temporarily unavailable due to coronavirus (COVID-19). You may be able to ask your GP for advice in the app instead. [Read more about appointments and online consultations](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/appointments-and-online-consultations-in-the-nhs-app/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).  
  
If you've been referred to a specialist through the NHS e-Referral Service, you can manage your first appointment in the app. [Read more about hospital and other appointments](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/appointments-and-online-consultations-in-the-nhs-app/hospital-and-other-appointments/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).  
  
**Linked profiles**  
  
Access health services in the app on behalf of someone else, like your child.  
  
To link a profile:  
  
1. Contact the GP surgery of the person you would like to set up a linked profile for.  
2. Ask them to register you for proxy access.  
  
[Read more about linked profiles](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/linked-profiles-in-the-nhs-app/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621).  
  
**Get help with the NHS App**  
  
Select the help icon from wherever you are in the app, to see a help page about that screen. For example, selecting the help icon from the prescriptions area will take you to a help page about prescriptions.

[See our help and support](https://nhs.uk/nhs-app/nhs-app-help-and-support/?wt.mc_id=intro_v1_0621)
